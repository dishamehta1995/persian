Experience the Royalty

Established on 13th June 1976, founded by 5 friends who were avid food lovers. Knowing their love for food they decided why not start a restaurant of their own. That was the inception of Persian Darbar.

Being a pioneer in the various cuisine specialities like Mughlai, Punjabi, Chinese, Sea cuisines made Persian Darbar the most loved restaurant in the city. Raan being their signature dish available in more than 10 varieties captured more hearts, that we call our self the Raan King.

In 1999 the second Generation Mr. Javed Kotwala and Mr. Faizal Bakshi stepped into the business growing it and taking it to heights and creating new bench marks. Through constant innovation and consistent growth, today we are among the fastest growing restaurant in Mumbai.

Winning many awards, to being celebs favourite along with many professional laurels, the praises and loyalty of its customers has always been the most integral part of our journey.

Applying new thoughts and ideas by renovating the restaurant and creating good ambience. Persian Darbar has established itself as a leader in food fine dining. Establishing the first specialized food court with different levels of dining and each floor having a unique touch to it makes Persian Darbar exclusive, luxurious, yet cost friendly.

As Persian Darbar, we welcome each one of you to come Dine and have an experience like never before!

Always ready to serve!
